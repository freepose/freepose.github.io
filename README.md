## Bio

**ZHIJIN WANG** is an associate professor at Jimei University, Xiamen China. He received his Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University, Shanghai, China, in 2016. He has published over 40 research papers in refereed international conferences and journals and applied for over 20 patents in China. His research interests include recommendation systems, time series forecasting, and artificial intelligence in health/healthcare.
